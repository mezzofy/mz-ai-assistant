# Finance Report Formulas

## Profit & Loss Statement
- Gross Profit = Revenue - Cost of Goods Sold
- EBITDA = Gross Profit - Operating Expenses + Depreciation + Amortization
- EBIT = EBITDA - Depreciation - Amortization
- Net Profit = EBIT - Interest - Tax

## Balance Sheet
- Total Assets = Current Assets + Non-Current Assets
- Total Liabilities = Current Liabilities + Non-Current Liabilities
- Equity = Total Assets - Total Liabilities
- Retained Earnings = Opening Retained Earnings + Net Profit - Dividends

## Cash Flow (Indirect Method)
- Operating Cash Flow = Net Profit + Non-Cash Items (Depreciation) ± Working Capital Changes
- Investing Cash Flow = Proceeds from asset sales - Capital expenditure
- Financing Cash Flow = Proceeds from loans - Loan repayments ± Equity changes
- Net Cash Flow = Operating + Investing + Financing

## Key Financial Ratios
- Current Ratio = Current Assets / Current Liabilities (target: >2)
- Quick Ratio = (Current Assets - Inventory) / Current Liabilities (target: >1)
- Debt/Equity = Total Debt / Shareholders Equity (lower is better)
- Gross Margin = Gross Profit / Revenue × 100%
- Net Margin = Net Profit / Revenue × 100%
- ROE = Net Profit / Shareholders Equity × 100%
- DSO (Days Sales Outstanding) = AR / (Revenue / 365)
- DPO (Days Payable Outstanding) = AP / (COGS / 365)

## AR Aging Buckets
- Current: Not yet due (due_date >= today)
- 1-30 days: 1 to 30 days overdue
- 31-60 days: 31 to 60 days overdue
- 61-90 days: 61 to 90 days overdue
- 90+ days: More than 90 days overdue (high credit risk — escalate immediately)

## GST Calculation
- Output Tax = Taxable Sale Amount × GST Rate (9%)
- Input Tax = Taxable Purchase Amount × GST Rate (where claimable)
- Net GST Payable = Total Output Tax - Total Claimable Input Tax
- If Net GST Payable < 0 → GST Refund from IRAS

## Depreciation Methods (Singapore)
- Straight-Line: (Cost - Residual Value) / Useful Life
- Declining Balance: Net Book Value × Depreciation Rate
- Standard useful lives: IT equipment 3yr, Furniture 5yr, Vehicles 5yr, Leasehold improvements = lease term

## Intercompany Elimination (Group Consolidation)
- Eliminate intercompany revenue/cost pairs
- Eliminate intercompany AR/AP balances
- Eliminate investment in subsidiary against subsidiary equity
- Recognise minority interest where applicable
