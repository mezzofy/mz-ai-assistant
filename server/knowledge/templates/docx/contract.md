# Template: Contract / Agreement (DOCX)

## Purpose
Legal or commercial agreements between Mezzofy and a counterparty (client, vendor, partner).

## Document Structure

1. **Header** — Mezzofy logo area, document title (e.g., "Service Agreement"), reference number, date.
2. **Parties** — Full legal names, registered addresses, and defined short names (e.g., "the Company", "the Client").
3. **Recitals / Background** — Brief context: why the parties are entering this agreement (WHEREAS clauses).
4. **Definitions** — Alphabetical list of all capitalised terms used in the agreement.
5. **Scope of Services / Subject Matter** — What is being agreed; deliverables, timelines, service levels.
6. **Fees & Payment Terms** — Amounts, currency, payment schedule, late payment clauses.
7. **Intellectual Property** — Ownership of work product, licence grants, restrictions.
8. **Confidentiality** — What is confidential, duration, permitted disclosures.
9. **Representations & Warranties** — Each party's representations about authority, compliance, accuracy.
10. **Limitation of Liability** — Caps, exclusions, consequential damages waiver.
11. **Term & Termination** — Contract duration, renewal, termination triggers, notice periods.
12. **General / Boilerplate** — Governing law, dispute resolution, entire agreement, severability, amendments.
13. **Signature Block** — Name, title, signature line, date — for each party.

## Tone
Formal and precise. Avoid ambiguous language. Define every term on first use.

## Formatting Notes
- Section numbering: 1., 1.1, 1.1.1
- Defined terms in Title Case throughout
- Page numbers in footer: "Page N of M"
