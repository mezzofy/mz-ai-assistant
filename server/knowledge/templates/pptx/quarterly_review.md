# Template: Quarterly Business Review (PPTX)

## Purpose
Internal or executive quarterly performance review. Use for QBR meetings, board updates, or team retrospectives.

## Slide Structure

1. **Cover** — "Q[N] [Year] Business Review", department/team name, date. Black background.
2. **Executive Summary** — 3–5 bullet highlights of the quarter. Green/red indicators for vs target.
3. **KPI Dashboard** — Key metrics vs targets vs prior quarter. Use tables or charts.
4. **Revenue & Growth** — Revenue trend, MoM/QoQ growth, cohort performance.
5. **Operational Highlights** — Major milestones, launches, partnerships delivered.
6. **Customer Metrics** — Acquisition, retention, NPS, churn. Trend lines.
7. **Challenges & Risks** — What didn't go well, root causes, mitigation plans.
8. **Next Quarter Priorities** — Top 3–5 objectives with owners and due dates.
9. **Resource Requests** — Headcount, budget, tooling needs if applicable.
10. **Appendix** — Supporting data, detailed breakdowns (label each slide "Appendix – [topic]").

## Tone
Objective and direct. Acknowledge misses honestly; focus on learnings and forward plan.

## Design Notes
- Use red (#ef4444) sparingly for "below target" indicators only
- Use green (#22c55e) sparingly for "above target" indicators only
- All other accents: Mezzofy orange (#f97316)
