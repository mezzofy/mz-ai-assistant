# Template: Pitch Deck (PPTX)

## Purpose
Investor or partner pitch presentation. Use for fundraising, partnership proposals, or new-product introductions.

## Slide Structure

1. **Cover** — Company name, tagline, presenter name, date. Black background, orange accents.
2. **Problem** — The market pain point being solved. 2–3 bullet points max. Use data.
3. **Solution** — How Mezzofy solves it. One clear value proposition sentence + supporting points.
4. **Market Opportunity** — TAM / SAM / SOM. Include a simple chart or visual.
5. **Product / Platform** — Key features, screenshot or diagram. Focus on outcomes not features.
6. **Business Model** — How revenue is generated. Pricing tiers if relevant.
7. **Traction** — Key metrics: users, GMV, growth %, notable clients. Use numbers.
8. **Team** — Founders and key hires. Photo, name, title, one-line credential each.
9. **Ask / Call to Action** — What you want from the audience. Be specific.
10. **Thank You** — Contact info, website. Black background, orange logo area.

## Tone
Confident, data-driven, concise. Every slide must answer: "So what?"

## Design Notes
- Max 6 words per headline
- One key idea per slide
- Use orange for emphasis highlights within body text
