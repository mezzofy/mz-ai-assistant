# Template: KPI Dashboard Report (PDF)

## Purpose
Periodic KPI and performance dashboard exported as PDF. Used for management reporting, investor updates, or automated scheduled reports.

## Document Structure

1. **Header** — "Mezzofy KPI Dashboard", reporting period (e.g., "March 2026"), generated date.
2. **Overview / Scorecard** — Summary table: metric name | target | actual | variance | status (✅/⚠️/❌).
3. **Revenue Metrics** — MRR, ARR, GMV, ARPU, revenue growth %. Include period-over-period trend.
4. **User & Engagement Metrics** — DAU/MAU, new signups, retention rate, churn rate.
5. **Operational Metrics** — SLA compliance, uptime, response times, error rates.
6. **Sales & Marketing** — Pipeline value, conversion rates, CAC, LTV, campaign performance.
7. **Financial Summary** — P&L highlights, burn rate, runway (if applicable).
8. **Exceptions & Actions** — Metrics below threshold: root cause, owner, action, due date.
9. **Footnotes** — Data sources, calculation methodology, period definitions.

## Tone
Factual and neutral. Numbers speak for themselves — add brief commentary only for significant variances.

## Formatting Notes
- Status indicators: ✅ ≥100% of target, ⚠️ 80–99%, ❌ <80%
- All currency in USD unless specified
- Percentages to 1 decimal place; monetary values to nearest integer
- Orange header rows on all tables; alternate light orange rows
