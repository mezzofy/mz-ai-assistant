# Dispute Resolution Clauses — Standard Library

## 1. TIERED DISPUTE RESOLUTION (RECOMMENDED)
In the event of any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement, the Parties shall attempt to resolve the dispute through the following tiered process:
**Tier 1 — Negotiation:** The Parties shall negotiate in good faith for [30] days after written notice of dispute.
**Tier 2 — Mediation:** If unresolved, the Parties shall submit to mediation administered by [MEDIATION CENTRE] for [30] days.
**Tier 3 — Arbitration/Litigation:** If mediation fails, proceed per Clause [X] below.

## 2. SIAC ARBITRATION (Singapore)
Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement, including any question regarding its existence, validity, or termination, shall be referred to and finally resolved by arbitration administered by the Singapore International Arbitration Centre ("SIAC") in accordance with the Arbitration Rules of the SIAC for the time being in force, which rules are deemed to be incorporated by reference in this clause. The seat of arbitration shall be Singapore. The Tribunal shall consist of [1 / 3] arbitrator(s). The language of arbitration shall be English.

## 3. HKIAC ARBITRATION (Hong Kong)
Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be referred to and finally resolved by arbitration administered by the Hong Kong International Arbitration Centre ("HKIAC") in accordance with the HKIAC Administered Arbitration Rules in force at the time. The seat of arbitration shall be Hong Kong. The Tribunal shall consist of [1 / 3] arbitrator(s). Language: English.

## 4. DIFC-LCIA ARBITRATION (UAE/DIFC)
Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be referred to and finally resolved by arbitration under the DIFC-LCIA Arbitration Rules, which are deemed incorporated by reference. The seat of arbitration shall be the DIFC, Dubai. The number of arbitrators shall be [1 / 3]. Language: English.

## 5. DIAC ARBITRATION (UAE Onshore)
Any dispute shall be referred to and finally resolved by arbitration before the Dubai International Arbitration Centre (DIAC) under its Arbitration Rules. Seat: Dubai. Language: [Arabic / English / Bilingual].

## 6. SINGAPORE COURTS (Exclusive Jurisdiction)
This Agreement is governed by Singapore law. Each Party irrevocably submits to the exclusive jurisdiction of the Singapore courts.

## 7. HONG KONG COURTS
This Agreement is governed by Hong Kong law. Each Party irrevocably submits to the exclusive jurisdiction of the Hong Kong courts.

## Notes for Leo
- Arbitration recommended over litigation for cross-border contracts (New York Convention enforcement in 160+ countries)
- SIAC: leading Asia arbitration seat; SIAC Emergency Arbitrator available
- HKIAC: strong for China-related disputes; HKIAC awards enforceable in PRC under HK-China arrangement
- DIAC/DIFC-LCIA: DIAC for UAE onshore; DIFC-LCIA for DIFC entities (DIFC-LCIA merged into DIFC Arbitration Institute in 2021)
- SCCA: preferred for Saudi Arabia contracts; Sharia compliance required for enforcement
- QICCA: preferred for Qatar contracts; QFC entities use QFC Dispute Resolution Centre
