## 1. DEFINITIONS
"**Confidential Information**" means any and all information disclosed by the Disclosing Party to the Receiving Party whether in written, oral, electronic, or any other form, including without limitation business plans, financial information, technical data, customer lists, trade secrets, and any other proprietary information, that is marked as confidential or that a reasonable person would understand to be confidential in the circumstances of disclosure.

## 2. CONFIDENTIALITY OBLIGATIONS
2.1 The Receiving Party shall:
(a) hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence;
(b) not disclose any Confidential Information to any third party without prior written consent;
(c) use the Confidential Information solely for [PURPOSE];
(d) apply at least the same standard of care to Confidential Information as it applies to its own confidential information, but in no event less than reasonable care;
(e) restrict access to the Confidential Information to those employees, contractors, and professional advisers who have a need to know and are bound by obligations of confidentiality no less stringent than those in this Agreement.

2.2 The obligations in Clause 2.1 do not apply to information that:
(a) is or becomes publicly available through no act or omission of the Receiving Party;
(b) the Receiving Party can demonstrate was already known to it prior to disclosure;
(c) is lawfully received from a third party free of restriction; or
(d) is required to be disclosed by applicable law or court order (provided prompt prior notice is given to the Disclosing Party).

## 3. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
Nothing in this Agreement transfers any intellectual property rights. The Disclosing Party retains all rights, title, and interest in the Confidential Information.

## 4. TERM AND SURVIVAL
4.1 This Agreement takes effect on the Effective Date and continues for [DURATION, e.g., 2 years].
4.2 The obligations of confidentiality survive termination of this Agreement for a further period of [SURVIVAL PERIOD, e.g., 3 years].

## 5. RETURN OR DESTRUCTION
Upon written request or expiry of this Agreement, the Receiving Party shall promptly return or securely destroy all Confidential Information and confirm the same in writing.

## 6. REMEDIES
The Receiving Party acknowledges that breach or threatened breach of this Agreement will cause irreparable harm, and the Disclosing Party shall be entitled to seek injunctive or other equitable relief in addition to all other available remedies.

## 7. GENERAL
7.1 **Governing Law:** This Agreement is governed by the laws of the Republic of Singapore.
7.2 **Dispute Resolution:** Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement, including any question regarding its existence, validity, or termination, shall be submitted to the exclusive jurisdiction of the Singapore courts [OR: referred to and finally resolved by arbitration administered by the Singapore International Arbitration Centre (SIAC) in accordance with the Arbitration Rules of SIAC for the time being in force].
7.3 **Electronic Signatures:** Signatures by electronic means are valid under the Electronic Transactions Act (Cap. 88).
7.4 **Entire Agreement:** This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties on its subject matter.
7.5 **Severability:** If any provision is held invalid, it shall be severed and the remaining provisions shall continue in full force.
