# NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT (NDA)
## General Template — English Law

**PARTIES:**
This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of [EFFECTIVE DATE]

**BETWEEN:** [PARTY A], a company incorporated in [PARTY A JURISDICTION] with registration number [REG NO A] ("Disclosing Party")

**AND:** [PARTY B], a company incorporated in [PARTY B JURISDICTION] with registration number [REG NO B] ("Receiving Party")

(Each a "Party" and together the "Parties")

---

## 1. BACKGROUND
The Parties wish to explore [COMMERCIAL PURPOSE] ("Purpose") and, in connection therewith, the Disclosing Party may disclose Confidential Information to the Receiving Party.

## 2. DEFINITIONS
2.1 **"Confidential Information"** means any information disclosed by the Disclosing Party to the Receiving Party in connection with the Purpose, whether in oral, written, electronic, or any other form, that is designated as confidential or that reasonably should be understood to be confidential given the nature of the information and the circumstances of disclosure.

2.2 Confidential Information does not include information that:
(a) is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Receiving Party;
(b) was rightfully known to the Receiving Party prior to disclosure;
(c) is received from a third party without restriction; or
(d) is independently developed by the Receiving Party without use of the Confidential Information.

## 3. OBLIGATIONS
3.1 The Receiving Party shall:
(a) keep the Confidential Information strictly confidential;
(b) not disclose the Confidential Information to any third party without the Disclosing Party's prior written consent;
(c) use the Confidential Information solely for the Purpose;
(d) protect the Confidential Information using at least the same degree of care it uses for its own confidential information, but no less than reasonable care;
(e) limit access to the Confidential Information to its employees, officers, and advisers who have a need to know for the Purpose and are bound by confidentiality obligations no less restrictive than this Agreement.

## 4. TERM
This Agreement shall remain in effect for [DURATION, e.g., 3 years] from the Effective Date, unless terminated earlier by mutual written agreement. The confidentiality obligations shall survive termination for [SURVIVAL PERIOD, e.g., 5 years].

## 5. RETURN OF INFORMATION
Upon written request, the Receiving Party shall promptly return or destroy all Confidential Information and certify such destruction in writing.

## 6. REMEDIES
The Receiving Party acknowledges that breach of this Agreement would cause irreparable harm for which monetary damages would be an inadequate remedy and that the Disclosing Party shall be entitled to seek injunctive relief in addition to any other remedies available.

## 7. NO LICENCE
Nothing in this Agreement grants the Receiving Party any right, title, or interest in or to the Confidential Information, or any licence to use the Confidential Information other than for the Purpose.

## 8. GOVERNING LAW AND JURISDICTION
This Agreement is governed by the laws of [GOVERNING LAW, e.g., England and Wales]. Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of [JURISDICTION].

## 9. ENTIRE AGREEMENT
This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties with respect to its subject matter and supersedes all prior agreements, understandings, and negotiations.

## 10. AMENDMENT
This Agreement may only be amended by written instrument signed by both Parties.
