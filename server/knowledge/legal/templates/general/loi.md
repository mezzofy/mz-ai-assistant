## 1. PROPOSED TRANSACTION
[PARTY A] proposes to [DESCRIBE TRANSACTION — e.g., acquire 60% of the issued share capital of the Company from the existing shareholders] ("Transaction") on the following indicative terms:

- **Transaction Value / Consideration:** [AMOUNT / VALUATION BASIS]

- **Conditions Precedent:** [e.g., satisfactory due diligence, regulatory approval, board approval]

## 2. DUE DILIGENCE
[PARTY A] intends to conduct legal, financial, tax, and commercial due diligence on the Company. [PARTY B] agrees to provide reasonable access to documents, personnel, and records reasonably required for this process. Due diligence to be completed within [TIMEFRAME].

## 3. EXCLUSIVITY (BINDING)
For a period of [e.g., 60] days from the date of this LOI ("Exclusivity Period"), [PARTY B] shall not, and shall procure that its shareholders, directors, and advisers shall not, directly or indirectly:
(a) solicit, discuss, or entertain proposals from any third party regarding a transaction similar to the Transaction; or
(b) enter into any agreement, understanding, or arrangement with any third party in connection with such a transaction.

## 4. CONFIDENTIALITY (BINDING)
The existence and contents of this LOI and all discussions between the Parties in connection with the Transaction shall be kept strictly confidential and shall not be disclosed to any third party without the prior written consent of the other Party, except to professional advisers on a need-to-know basis.

## 5. NON-BINDING NATURE
Except for Clauses 3, 4, 5, 6, and 7 (which are legally binding), this LOI is a statement of intent only and does not create legally binding obligations on either Party. The Transaction is subject to:
(a) completion of satisfactory due diligence;
(b) agreement and execution of definitive transaction documents; and
(c) [any other conditions].

## 6. COSTS (BINDING)
Each Party shall bear its own costs and expenses in connection with the Transaction, unless otherwise agreed.

## 7. GOVERNING LAW (BINDING)
This LOI (and the binding provisions hereof) is governed by the laws of [GOVERNING LAW].

## 8. EXPIRY
This LOI shall expire if definitive agreements have not been signed by [LONG STOP DATE], unless extended by written agreement.
