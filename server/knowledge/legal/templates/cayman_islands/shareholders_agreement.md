4.1 **Lock-up:** Founders may not transfer shares for [24] months without Investor consent.

## 5. DRAG-ALONG RIGHTS
If shareholders holding [75%] of shares approve a sale, all other shareholders are obliged to sell on the same terms.

## 6. TAG-ALONG RIGHTS
If any shareholder proposes to sell shares to a third party, minority shareholders have the right to join the sale on the same terms.

## 7. ANTI-DILUTION

## 8. INFORMATION RIGHTS
The Company shall provide to the Investor: (a) monthly management accounts within 30 days; (b) audited annual accounts within 90 days; (c) annual budget within 30 days of year-end; (d) notice of material events promptly.

## 9. CONFIDENTIALITY
Each Party shall keep confidential the terms of this Agreement and all non-public Company information.
