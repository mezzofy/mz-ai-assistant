## Jurisdictions Covered

| Jurisdiction | Legal System | Arbitration | Key Regulator |
|---|---|---|---|
| Singapore | Common Law | SIAC | ACRA, MAS, MOM |
| Hong Kong | Common Law | HKIAC | CR, SFC, HKMA |
| Malaysia | Common Law + Syariah | AIAC | SSM, BNM |
| UAE (incl. DIFC/ADGM) | Civil + Common Law (free zones) | DIAC / DIFC-LCIA | DED, CBUAE, DFSA |
| Saudi Arabia | Islamic Law + Royal Decrees | SCCA | MISA, SAMA, CMA |
| Qatar (incl. QFC) | Civil + Common Law (QFC) | QICCA | MOCI, QCB |
| Cayman Islands | Common Law | Grand Court / London | CIMA |
