# Data Protection Law Comparison
## PDPA SG vs PDPO HK vs PDPA MY vs UAE PDPL vs DIFC DP Law vs Saudi PDPL

| Feature | SG PDPA 2012 | HK PDPO (Cap 486) | MY PDPA 2010 | UAE PDPL 2021 | DIFC DP Law 2020 | Saudi PDPL 2021 |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Effective** | 2014 (amended 2020) | 1996 (amended) | 2013 | Sep 2023 | Jul 2020 | Sep 2023 |
| **Regulator** | PDPC | PCPD | PDPD Commissioner | UAE Data Office | DIFC Commissioner of Data Protection | SDAIA/NDMO |
| **Scope** | Commercial orgs in SG | Any person (SG-based data) | Commercial transactions in MY | All processing in UAE (excl. govt) | DIFC entities | Personal data of individuals in KSA |
| **Legal basis** | Consent (primarily) + exceptions | Consent + exceptions | Consent (primarily) | Consent + 5 other legal bases | Consent + 5 GDPR-aligned bases | Consent + exceptions |
| **DPO required** | Not mandatory (recommended) | Not mandatory | Not mandatory | Not mandatory (recommended) | Required for certain controllers | Not mandatory |
| **Breach notification** | 3 business days to PDPC (if >500 or significant harm) | No statutory obligation | No statutory obligation | 72 hours to UAE Data Office | 72 hours to Commissioner | 72 hours to SDAIA |
| **Cross-border transfer** | Approved countries list | PDPO transfer restriction applies | Gazetted countries only | Adequate countries or safeguards | Adequate countries or safeguards | Adequate countries or contractual safeguards |
| **Max penalty** | SGD 1M or 10% of annual SG turnover | HKD 50,000 + imprisonment | MYR 500,000 + 3 years imprisonment | AED 5M (administrative) | USD 100K - USD 100M (case-dependent) | SAR 5M |
| **Right to deletion** | Yes (under consent withdrawal) | Yes (inaccuracy-based) | Limited | Yes (explicit right) | Yes (GDPR-aligned) | Yes |
| **GDPR alignment** | Partial | Partial | Partial | Partial | Full GDPR alignment | Partial |

## Key Observations for Leo

### For SG-based contracts:
PDPA is the primary law. Data sharing agreements (DSAs) recommended for B2B data sharing.
PDPA notification exceptions: research, legitimate interests (limited), legally required.

### For HK-based contracts:
PDPO applies. No GDPR equivalent — older regime. Recommended to include data processing agreements.
PCPD has issued guidance on AI and data governance.

### For MY-based contracts:
PDPA MY does not apply to federal/state government. Relatively narrow scope vs GDPR.
Transfer restriction: only to approved (gazetted) countries — list is limited; obtain consent for others.

### For UAE contracts (onshore):
UAE PDPL applies to all private sector processing. Government exempt.
DIFC entities: DIFC DP Law applies (more stringent, GDPR-aligned).
Both laws may apply simultaneously if entity is both onshore and DIFC-registered.

### For Saudi Arabia contracts:
PDPL applies. SDAIA is active regulator. Executive Regulations provide implementation detail.
Cross-border restrictions are strict — verify adequacy or obtain appropriate safeguards.

### Multi-jurisdiction contracts:
Apply the most stringent standard (usually DIFC or Saudi PDPL) to be safe.
Include governing law for data protection clause and specify the applicable law.
